Our Story

A Neighborhood Tradition Since 1987

We started in the fall of '87. The community has changed over the years but the quality of food has not. When Fort Monmouth closed, many left, but our community remained loyal customers. The support always survived.

Sal Martorana was the one that started it all. Back in March 2024, the McIntyres decided to take it over. Since then, Sean McIntyre has been serving the Jersey Shore with his 10+ years of experience and love for the diner atmosphere. When you come here, you're a part of the family.
